Ghost has been deployed!

1. Get the Ghost URL:
   kubectl get svc {{ .Release.Name }}-ghost -n {{ .Release.Namespace }}

2. Wait for EXTERNAL-IP to be assigned (1-2 minutes)

3. Update ghost.url in values.yaml with the ELB DNS:
   ghost:
     url: "http://<EXTERNAL-IP>"

4. Access Ghost admin:
   http://<EXTERNAL-IP>/ghost
